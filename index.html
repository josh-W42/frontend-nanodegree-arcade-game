Welcome to the game!

Instructions:

This goal of this game is to cross the bug filled street to reach the water. However, these bugs are DANGEROUS and will attack, making you lose a life. If you happen to lose all of your lives, you will LOSE. But, if you reach a score of 5000, you will WIN.

If you're lucky, you could find valuable gems that increase your score. But Beware! they can be risky to capture, and your goal is still to reach the water.

Play once on easy mode to get a hang of the game, then choose your own difficulty.

Have Fun!
